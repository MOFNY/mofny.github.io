The Definitive Roy Oswalt Collection

Hello fellow collectors, my name is Chris. This site is dedicated to displaying my Roy Oswalt, Daniel Hudson, Tim Hudson, Dan Haren, and RC collections. I've collected Oswalt for over ten years, amassing 1,247 uniquely different cards. Put simply, this is the greatest resource for collectors of Roy Oswalt. Please use the contact form to inquire about anything. This site functions optimally when viewed in Chrome, Firefox, or IE10.
