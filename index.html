Hello fellow collectors, my name is Chris. This site is dedicated to displaying my Roy Oswalt collection. I've collected Oswalt for over ten years, amassing 1,245 uniquely different cards. Put simply, this is the greatest resource for collectors of Roy Oswalt. Please use the contact form in the footer to inquire about anything. I also collect Tim Hudson and Jordan Zimmermann. The site functions optimally when viewed in Chrome, Firefox, or IE10.
Intrigued by the numbers above? They are the number of uniquely different cards in each category. Hover over or click each category to see the individual completion statistics. How did I estimate such lavish ratios? Beckett is the first step. Then I eliminate the cards I presume undesirable: minor league issues, show stamped and numbered (such as Hawaii or Chicago), and certain aftermarket cards. Each page contains high quality scans.

This is the first blog posting for the new site. Recently, I decided to do away with Weebly for hosting. I highly recommend Github Pages for free hosting. Coming soon will be a redesigned site for my Roy Oswalt collection. I will also be adding information on my appraisal service and galleries for my investment cards. Please be patient during the transition.

About the site creator

Most of what you see is original CSS/HTML/JS by yours truly. It can all be viewed on my Github repistory. I reside in Canandaigua, NY. Outside of collecting, my passion is web programming. Check out my Linkedin profile for more details. I also collect Tim Hudson, Jordan Zimmermann, and Dan Haren.
Also, I created a Player Collector's Compilation which brings together collectors with completion percentages. Joinging is extremely easy: having a personal site is key (but not necessary), and having your completion percentages up to date. Use my form to inquire about joining.
