Hello fellow collectors, my name is Chris. This site is dedicated to displaying my Roy Oswalt, Daniel Hudson, Tim Hudson, Dan Haren, and RC collections. I've collected Oswalt for over ten years, amassing 1,245 uniquely different cards. Put simply, this is the greatest resource for collectors of Roy Oswalt. Please use the contact form to inquire about anything. This site functions optimally when viewed in Chrome, Firefox, or IE10.
Intrigued by those numbers up there? They represent the number of uniquely different cards in each category. Hover over each category to see the individual completion statistics. How did I estimate such lavish statistics? Beckett is the first step. Then I eliminate the cards I don't collect: minor league issues, show stamped and numbered (such as Hawaii or Chicago), and certain aftermarket cards. Each page features high quality scans!
