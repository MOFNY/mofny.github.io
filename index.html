A brief intro

Hello fellow collectors, my name is Chris. This site is dedicated to displaying my Roy Oswalt collection. I've collected Oswalt for over ten years, amassing 1,600 uniquely different cards. Put simply, this is the greatest resource for collectors of Roy Oswalt. Please use the contact form in the footer to inquire about anything. I also collect Tim Hudson and Jordan Zimmermann. The collection is updated often, with highlights listed below.

What about the numbers?

Intrigued by the statistics above? They are the number of uniquely different cards in each category. Hover over or click each category to see the individual completion statistics. How did I estimate such lavish ratios? Beckett is the first step. Then I eliminate the cards I presume undesirable: oversized, minor league issues, show stamped and numbered (such as Hawaii or Chicago), and certain aftermarket cards. Each page contains high quality scans.

About the site creator

My primary collecting habits revolve around Roy Oswalt. The first card was purchased in 2003. I'm always looking to acquire new cards from 2000 to 2012. Please use the contact form, or send a message via eBay or FreedomCardBoard.

For my scans I use a CanoScan 9000F Mark 2. If you prefer an alternative viewing experience, high quality and high resolution images are available on Flikr.

Most of what you see is original CSS/HTML/JS by yours truly. It can all be viewed on my Github repository. I reside in East Greenwich, RI. Outside of collecting, my passion is web development and design. Check out my Codepen for more details. I also collect Tim Hudson, Jordan Zimmermann, and Dan Haren.
