Hello fellow collectors, my name is Chris. This site is dedicated to displaying my Roy Oswalt collection. I've collected Oswalt for over ten years, amassing 1,408 uniquely different cards. Put simply, this is the greatest resource for collectors of Roy Oswalt. Please use the contact form in the footer to inquire about anything. I also collect Tim Hudson and Jordan Zimmermann. The collection is updated often, with highlights listed below.
Intrigued by the numbers above? They are the number of uniquely different cards in each category. Hover over or click each category to see the individual completion statistics. How did I estimate such lavish ratios? Beckett is the first step. Then I eliminate the cards I presume undesirable: minor league issues, show stamped and numbered (such as Hawaii or Chicago), and certain aftermarket cards. Each page contains high quality scans.

Card Additions & Site Updates

May 6, 2017

I have reached the second big milestone of 2017: card #1,400! Several new scans of superfractors were added as well.

April 16, 2017

A few pages have been updated for better usability. Now it's much easier to scroll through a page, or just jump to a specific year. Also, over 50 new cards have been added since the last update. I'm now 102 cards away from 1,500! The game used category now includes cards from 2012. All totals have been adjusted to include 7 new cards.

March 15, 2017

After roughly 14 years of collecting Oswalt, I've hit over 1,300 cards. The large category of base, inserts, and parallels has also hit over 1,000 cards. It has been a labor of love to amass so many cards. The next goal is reach to the prestigious 1,500 club.

February 26, 2017

My new scanner is here, specifically the Canoscan 9000F Mark 2. The quality is fantastic. See it in action for my 30th 1/1 from 2005 Reflections.

January 28, 2017

The domain for this site has finally been transferred! I will no longer be updating the old site. I've continued to make improvements to some old code. New cards will also be added soon.

January 2, 2017

I've added 12 new base and parallels. This brings that section up to over 50% completion, making it the second to reach that distinction.

December 17, 2016

I've made many style improvements and bug fixes recently. I'm adding a new page featuring all cards in one giant list. Additionally, there will be around 80 new cards added soon, bringing the total to over 1,300! The majority are parallels, but I was able to acquire some game used.

August 27, 2016

It's been awhile, but I'm back!

September 2, 2014

This is the first blog posting for the new site. Recently, I decided to do away with Weebly for hosting. I highly recommend Github Pages for free hosting. This site is a work in progress, so some aspects will not be perfect. However, I'm working daily to get things in order. In the footer you will find a contact form.

February 25, 2011.

Coming soon will be a redesigned site for my Roy Oswalt collection. I will also be adding information on my appraisal service and galleries for my investment cards. Please be patient during the transition.

About the site creator

Most of what you see is original CSS/HTML/JS by yours truly. It can all be viewed on my Github repository. I reside in Ithaca, NY. Outside of collecting, my passion is web development and design. Check out my Codepen for more details. I also collect Tim Hudson, Jordan Zimmermann, and Dan Haren.

My primary collecting habits revolve around Roy Oswalt. The first card was purchased in 2003. I'm always looking to acquire new cards from 2000 to 2012. Please use the contact form, or send a message via eBay or FreedomCardBoard.
