About the site creator

Most of what you see is original CSS/HTML/JS by yours truly. It can all be viewed on my Github repository. I reside in Ithaca, NY. Outside of collecting, my passion is web development and design. Check out my Codepen for more details. I also collect Tim Hudson, Jordan Zimmermann, and Dan Haren.

My primary collecting habits revolve around Roy Oswalt. The first card was purchased in 2003. I'm always looking to acquire new cards from 2000 to 2012. Please use the contact form, or send a message via eBay or FreedomCardBoard.
