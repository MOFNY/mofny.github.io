About the site creator

Most of what you see is original CSS/HTML/JS by yours truly. It can all be viewed on my Github repistory. I reside in Ithaca, NY. Outside of collecting, my passion is web development and design. Check out my Linkedin profile for more details. I also collect Tim Hudson, Jordan Zimmermann, and Dan Haren.
Also, I created a Player Collector's Compilation which brings together collectors with completion percentages. Joinging is extremely easy: having a personal site is key (but not necessary), and having your completion percentages up to date. Use my form to inquire about joining.
