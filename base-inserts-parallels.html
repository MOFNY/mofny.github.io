About the site creator

Since 2003, my personal collection has revolved around Roy Oswalt. I'm always looking to acquire new cards from 2000 to 2019. Please use the contact form, or send a message via eBay or FreedomCardBoard.

For my scans I use a CanoScan 9000F Mark 2. If you prefer an alternative viewing experience, high quality and high resolution images are available on Flikr.

Most of what you see is original CSS/HTML/JS by yours truly. It can all be viewed on my Github repository. Outside of collecting, my passion is web development and design. Check out my Codepen for more details.
